Our Comprehensive Cumulative Records Management System (CCRMS) is designed to streamline the management of student records, improve access to academic, counseling, and personal development data, and provide guidance counselors with tools to better understand and support student growth. By digitizing and integrating relevant student information, we aim to empower counselors, academic advisers, and administrators to make data-driven decisions that positively impact student outcomes.

Rationale

The need for an efficient, accessible, and user-friendly system to manage student records is crucial for improving guidance and counseling services. The current manual record-keeping system presents challenges in accessing, updating, and analyzing student data. Our CCRMS addresses these issues by digitizing records, making it easier for counselors and advisers to track student progress, academic performance, and personal development. This system not only reduces administrative workload but also enhances the quality of counseling interventions, leading to better support for students' academic and personal growth. By improving the management of student data, the CCRMS ultimately aims to contribute to higher student success rates and more effective guidance services.
